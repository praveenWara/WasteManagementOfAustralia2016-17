Food Waste Management Statistics of Australia 2016-17

Acording to the national waste report, Australia produced 5 Million tonnes of food waste comprised of 4.3 million tonnes of non hazardous food waste and 0.7 million tonnes of hazardous food waste. The reports cites that, Non hazardous food waste is mainly the core food waste discarded from house holds and businesses where as hazardous food waste includes grease trap sludges and wastes from abbattoirs and tanneris.The following chart depicts the management methods and percentages of waste dealt by each method.
