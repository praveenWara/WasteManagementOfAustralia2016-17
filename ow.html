Organic Waste Generation and Management Statistics of Australia 2016-17

Australia produced 30 million tonnes of organic waste in the year of 2016-17. The waste predominatly can be catagorised into following streams,

  1. C&I (agriculture & fisheries)
  2. C&I (core)
  3. C&D
  4. MSW
Following charts contain a break down of the organic waste generation by the stream and material catagory.
  • C&D - Construction and Demolition
  • C&I - Commercial and Industrial
  • MSW - Municipal Solid Waste
