Waste Generation and Management Statistics of Australia 2016-17

During the period of 2016-17, Australia produced a total of 113.6 million tonnes of waste including 66.8 million tonnes of general waste (54.5 Mt of core waste + 12.3 Mt of Ash) and 58.3 million tonnes of waste from electricity generation,agriculture and mining.

  • C&I - Commercial and Industrial
  • MSW - Municipal Solid Waste
